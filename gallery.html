Kabul

Kabul is known for its vibrant bazaars and centuries-old poetry traditions.

Traditional Food

Kabuli Pulao

Special Dress

Women often wear embroidered long dresses with wide sleeves,men wear perahan tunban.

Herat

Herat is famous for its Persian architecture and classical art.

Traditional Food

Mantu (steamed meat dumplings)

Special Dress

Known for more Persian-style embroidery.

Kandahar

Kandahar is a cradle of Pashtun culture, known for its poetry and music.

Traditional Food

Saji (whole lamb or chicken grilled on skewers)

Special Dress

Men often wear large white turbans, known as "Lungee", women wear bright traditional dresses.

Mazar-i-Sharif

Mazar is home to the Blue Mosque and hosts colorful Nowruz festivals.

Traditional Food

Qabili Palaw

Special Dress

Richly colored dresses with detailed embroidery for women. Men typically wear traditional clothing.

Panjshir

Panjshir's culture is shaped by its heroic history and mountainous lifestyle.

Traditional Food

Simple mountain dishes like Shorwa

Special Dress

Functional and warm clothes.

Badakhshan

Badakhshan is rich in lapis lazuli and home to ancient Pamiri traditions.

Traditional Food

Tawa Fry Fish, Chopan Kabob

Special Dress

Pamiri women wear long, colorful dresses.Men wear wool hats, vests, and heavy coats in colder weather.
